SetGridSize()

 Previous  Top  Next




It establishes separation of the Grid in design Window



====== Sintaxis ======





 SetGridSize( <nWidth>, <nHeight> ) --> nil 


 


 



====== Parámetros ======





 <nWidth> Width among the points of the Grid 


 


 


 


 <nHeight> Height among the points of the Grid 


 


 





====== Devuelve ======





 Nil 


 


 



Observations:





 In the IDE of FiveWin a Grid of points is used for to adjust the
controls in their design phase. These points they are magnetic so that
when dragging a control for the surface of the design window, the control
will go being adjusted to the next point in the grid. With this function
the separation is adjusted among points.
