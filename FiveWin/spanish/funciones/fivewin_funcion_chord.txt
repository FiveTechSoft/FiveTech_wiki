
====== Chord() ======

Chord()
Draws a chord
====== Sintaxis ======
 Chord( <hDC>, <nLeftRect>, <nTopRect>,<nRightRect>, <nBottomRect>,
<nXStartLine>, <nYStartLine>, <nXEndLine>, <nYEndLine> ) --> lSuccess 

====== Parámetros ======

 <hDC> Identifies the device context where to paint 
 <nLeftRect> The rect of the bounding rectangle where to place the chord. 
 <nTopRect> 
 <nRightRect> 
 <nBottomRect> 
 <nXStartLine> The origin and end of the line that intersects with the
ellipse forming 

 <nYStartLine > the chord. 
 <nXEndLine> 
 <nYEndLine> 

====== Devuelve ======
 <lSuccess> A logical value indicating if the operation was performed
successfully.
