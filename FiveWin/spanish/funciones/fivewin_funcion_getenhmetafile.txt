GetEnhMetaFile()
 Previous  Top  Next

Creates a handle that identifies the enhanced-format metafile stored in
the specified file.

====== Sintaxis ======

 


 GetEnhMetaFile( <cMetaFile> ) --> hEMF 


 


 
====== Parámetros ======


 <cMetaFile> The filename of an enhanced metafile. 


 


 
====== Devuelve ======


 <hEMF> The handle of the enhanced metafile. Zero in case of failure.
