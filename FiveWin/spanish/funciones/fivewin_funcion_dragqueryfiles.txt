DragQueryFiles()
 Previous  Top  Next

Retrieves the file names of dropped files that have resulted from a
successful drag-and-drop operation.

====== Sintaxis ======


 DragQueryFiles( <hDrop> ) --> aFiles 


 

====== Parámetros ======


 <hDrop> Handler of the drag and drop proccess 


 

====== Devuelve ======


 <aFiles> An array of names of the dropped files.
