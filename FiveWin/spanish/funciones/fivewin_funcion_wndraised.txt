Makes the border of a window look raised



====== Sintaxis ======





 WndRaised( <hWnd>, <hDC> ) --> nil 


 


 



====== Parámetros ======



 


 


 <hWnd> Identifies the window to make it look raised. 


 


 


 


 <hDC> The device context of the window. 


 


 



====== Devuelve ======





 nil
