
====== KeyToggle() ======
KeyToggle()

Toggle a certain key
====== Sintaxis ======
 KeyToggle( <nKey> ) --> nil 
====== Parámetros ======
 <nKey> nKey to Toggle 
====== Devuelve ======
 nil 

Observations

 KeyToggle Toggles the LEDs and BIOS flags for the NUMLOCK, CAPSLOCK and
SCROLL LOCK keys
