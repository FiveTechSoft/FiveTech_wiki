Hex2Str()


Hex2Str()
 Previous  Top  Next

Converts a hexadecimal representation of a string in its corresponding
ASCII format.

====== Sintaxis ======

 


 Hex2Str( <cHex> ) --> cString 


 


 
====== Parámetros ======


 <cHex> The hexadecimal string to convert. 


 


 
====== Devuelve ======


 <cString> The converted ASCII string. 


 


 
Sample :

 


 

 SAMPLES\TESTHEX2.PRG 


 

 


 
Source code:

 


 SOURCE\FUNCTION\STR2HEX.C
