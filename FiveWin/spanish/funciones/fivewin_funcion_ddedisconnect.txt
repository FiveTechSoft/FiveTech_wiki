
====== DDEDisconnect() ======

Terminates a DDE conversation

====== Sintaxis ======

 DDEDisconnect( <hConv> ) --> lSuccess 

====== Parámetros ======
 <hConv> Handle of the conversation 

====== Devuelve ======
 <lSuccess> .t. if the operation is successful.
