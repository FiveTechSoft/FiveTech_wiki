Returns the total height of the screen. 

====== Syntax: ======
 
 ScreenHeight() --> nHeight
   
  
====== Returns: ======

 <nHeight> The total height of the screen, including the height of the dock if it's on the top or bottom side of the screen.  
 
====== Sample: ======
 <code clipper>
 local nHeight := ScreenHeight() 
 </code>
