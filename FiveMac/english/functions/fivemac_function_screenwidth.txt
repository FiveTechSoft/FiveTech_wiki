Returns the total width of the screen. 

====== Syntax: ======
 
 ScreenWidth() --> nWidth
   
  
====== Returns: ======

 <nWidth> The total width of the screen, including the width of the dock if it's on the left or right side of the screen.  
 
====== Sample: ======
 <code clipper>
 local nWidth := ScreenWidth() 
 </code>
